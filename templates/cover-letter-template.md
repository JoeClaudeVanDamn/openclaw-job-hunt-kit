# Cover Letter Template

Use this template as the skeleton for every cover letter. The jobapplyer agent will customize it per application.

---

Hi [hiring manager name if known, otherwise omit salutation],

I'm a good fit for this role. Here's why I think that.

[2-3 sentences about your most relevant experience. Reference specific roles, metrics, and achievements from your CV. Keep it concrete — numbers and outcomes, not buzzwords.]

[1-2 sentences about what kind of environment you thrive in and what tools/methods you use. Connect to the job description if possible.]

[If applicable: 1 sentence about visa/sponsorship status. Be upfront rather than leaving it for later.]

Happy to chat if the role seems like a fit.

[Your name]
[Your email] | [Your phone]
[Your LinkedIn URL]

---

## Customization Guide

The agent will replace the bracketed sections above using data from:
- `profile.json` — work history, achievements, skills
- `cv.pdf` — specific metrics and bullet points
- The job description — to tailor the middle paragraph

### Rules
- Keep it 250-350 words
- No em dashes
- Sound human, not corporate
- First person throughout
- Opening line stays exactly: "I'm a good fit for this role. Here's why I think that."
- Mention visa/sponsorship upfront if applicable (configured in profile.json)
